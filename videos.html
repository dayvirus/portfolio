Stages of my audio and video equipment.

In 1993, I was turned 13. The only thing I wanted for my birthday was an RCA shoulder video recording device. Like most suburban teens I was in a rap group. We called ourselves Brain Damage. At the time we were young angry kids. We listened to Chi-Ali, NWA, and Gang Starr.

After I got the video recorder I was able to figure out how to copy VHS tapes. Then I would make video mixes with the help of two vcrs an audio mixer. There was a game changer in the mid 2000's a digital camera with WiFi capabilities. At the time I was very WiFi niave, but that would soon change.
